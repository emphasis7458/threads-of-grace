Remembering Mom

This is a private space for our family to share and preserve memories of Mom. Here you'll find stories she told us in her own words, stories she wrote over the years, and the video from her memorial service.

Most of the recorded conversations were from the last month of her life. She wanted these stories saved, and we're glad she did.

Stories Mom Told

Eleven stories from recorded conversations, shaped into narrative in her own words. Childhood memories, how she met Daddy, teaching at a two-room schoolhouse, and more.

Stories Mom Wrote

Stories Pat wrote herself over the years, drawn from a lifetime of memories and reflections. Her favorite toys, travels, recipes, and the wisdom she wanted to pass along.

Family History

Stories from a conversation with Audrey and Tom, October 2024. The O’Malley, Johnson, Horn, and McNabb lines—how they came to America, where they settled, and what they carried with them.

Contributing

If you have photos, stories, recordings, or other memories of Mom that you'd like to add to this collection, please email them to Larry. Include any preferences you have for how we present them—whether you'd like them attributed by name, and any context or captions you'd like included.

We'd especially love to gather more photos, additional stories or memories from family, and anything else that captures who she was.
