Threads of Grace

Weekly Meditations by Pat Horn

For over sixteen years, Pat Horn faithfully crafted weekly meditations on the Sunday lectionary readings, weaving together scripture, spiritual wisdom, and personal reflection. Her dedication to this practice resulted in an extraordinary legacy—nearly 850 meditations covering the entire liturgical calendar.

Each meditation, which she calls "Threads of Grace," invites readers to pause, reflect, and discover God's presence in the ordinary moments of life. Pat's gentle yet profound voice guides seekers toward deeper encounters with the Divine.

This website shares a selection of her meditations with those seeking spiritual nourishment and contemplative wisdom.

About This Collection

This collection features 272 meditations spanning December 2007 through May 2023, covering all seasons of the church year. Each meditation reflects on the appointed scripture readings and offers spiritual insights for living faithfully in today's world.

The meditations follow the three-year lectionary cycle (Years A, B, and C) used by many Christian traditions, offering reflections on Advent, Christmas, Epiphany, Lent, Easter, and Ordinary Time.

About Pat Horn

Pat Horn is a spiritual companion, retreat leader, and writer. She has led numerous retreats and quiet days and been published in Forward Day by Day and a variety of other publications. She wrote weekly meditations on the Eucharistic Lectionary for her ministry, "Threads of Grace," which are distributed to email subscribers.

Pat is an Associate of the Community of the Holy Spirit, a religious order for women in the Episcopal Church, and is a graduate of the Lay School at the Virginia Theological Seminary. She has served as a mentor for Sewanee's Education for Ministry program and has conducted several spiritual formation programs for the Tallahassee Bible Institute. She also gave spiritual formation workshops at The Women's Triennial during the General Convention in Minneapolis in 2003 and serves as a facilitator for Centering Prayer groups.

She lives at Westminster Oaks, a retirement community in Tallahassee, Florida.
