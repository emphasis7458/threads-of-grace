17 Years (2007–2024)

The three-year lectionary cycle means the same liturgical occasions recur regularly. Pat thoughtfully reused 32 essays a total of 34 times when the same readings returned, while also writing fresh reflections for many recurring occasions. This intentional curation reflects her pastoral wisdom in knowing when a message bears repeating.
