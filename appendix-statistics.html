16 Years (2007–2024)

The three-year lectionary cycle means the same liturgical occasions recur regularly. Pat thoughtfully reused 34 meditations when the same readings returned, while also writing fresh reflections for many recurring occasions. This intentional curation reflects her pastoral wisdom in knowing when a message bears repeating.
